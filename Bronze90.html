We are a global supplier and manufacturer of ferrous and non-ferrous metal/alloy powders, including Copper, Bronze, Stainless Steel, Nickel, and more. Our expertise extends to high-temperature sintering processes, specializing in Tungsten Heavy Alloy Components (Inno Tung). Serving diverse industries such as Powder Metallurgy, Diamond tools, Coatings, and more, we aim for self-reliance through innovative research and development.
