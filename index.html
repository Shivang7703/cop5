We are a global manufacturer and supplier of ferrous and non-ferrous metal/alloy powders, including Copper, Bronze, Stainless Steel, Nickel, Tin, Brass, and Diamond Tool Matrix Powders. Our product range also includes tungsten heavy alloy series produced via Powder Metallurgy (INNOTUNG). We cater to various industries like Powder Metallurgy, Diamond tools, Welding, Pharmaceutical, and more. Committed to customer success, we prioritize quality, consistency, and timely delivery. Our experienced technical team focuses on R&D to offer customized solutions and advanced technology to the powder metallurgy community.
